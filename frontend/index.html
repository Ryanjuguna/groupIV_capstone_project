Breast Cancer Prediction

Project Description

A clinician-friendly ML tool that helps prioritize patients for follow-up by estimating breast cancer risk from routine clinical data.

Goal: Support earlier detection while keeping false negatives low.

The Challenge

Limited access, long wait times, and constrained resources mean some high-risk patients wait too long for diagnostics. We aim to reduce that delay.

Our Approach

We build a lightweight, transparent ML model trained on curated clinical datasets. The model produces a risk score and concise explanations so clinicians can act confidently.
